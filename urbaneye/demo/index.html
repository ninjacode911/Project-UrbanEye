System Architecture

Four-Stage
Perception Pipeline

From synthetic simulation to real-time tracking — each stage is independently executable, fully tested, and reproducible on free GPUs.

STAGE 01
Synthetic Data
CARLA generates 50K+ auto-annotated frames across 144 scenario configurations with full weather and lighting control.

STAGE 02
Model Training
YOLOv11 fine-tuned with 3-layer domain adaptation — weather randomization, augmentation, and BDD100K mixed training.

Dual Tracking
ByteTrack for real-time speed (60+ FPS) with two-stage matching, DeepSORT for identity-persistent tracking with Re-ID.
